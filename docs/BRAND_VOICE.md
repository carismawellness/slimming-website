# Carisma Slimming — Brand Voice

**Persona:** Katya
**Audience:** Women 25+ in Malta

Katya speaks like a trusted friend who has receipts — warm, honest, and never
judging. The Carisma Slimming voice combines emotional understanding with
grounded, real results, and always treats the reader as someone who is not
broken, just in need of a clear plan and real support.

---

## Core Voice Principles (Pillars)

### 1. Compassionate Truth-Telling
Speak to women like a trusted friend who has receipts. Warm, never judging, but
honest about what it really takes to change. Acknowledge their struggle before
offering a solution.

### 2. Gentle Structure, Not Punishment
"You are not broken, you just need a clear plan and support." Don't glorify
restriction or hustle. Frame structure as caring guidance, not discipline or
punishment.

### 3. Evidence-Led, Emotion-Aware
Combine emotional understanding with grounded, real results. Lean on data, clear
explanations and proof, but always through a human, empathetic lens.

### 4. Safe, Shame-Free Space
Never talk down to weight, age, or appearance. Normalize struggle, slip-ups, and
starting again. Make them feel "I am not the only one" and "it is safe to try
again here."

### 5. Future-Focused, Present-Centered
Help them envision their next chapter while anchoring them in tiny wins today.
Less "fix your body," more "build the life you want to live in your body."

---

## Do

- **Validate experience:**
  - "You are not the only one who feels this way."
  - "If you have started over more times than you can count, this is for you."
- **Use calm, clear, concrete language:**
  - "Lose 5 to 10 kilos in a medically supervised, step-by-step program."
  - "We plan your meals, movement and check-ins together."
- **Normalize relapse and imperfection:**
  - "You did not fail. The plan failed you."
  - "We design something you can live with on a bad day, not just a perfect one."
- **Emphasize control and choice:**
  - "You choose what you eat. We show you how to make it work."
  - "Your plan adapts to weekends, stress, and real life."
- **Talk about how life feels, not just how the body looks:**
  - "Climb stairs without thinking about it."
  - "Wear the dress without checking angles in photos."

## Don't

- **Don't shame or blame.** Avoid "lack of discipline", "you let yourself go",
  "fix your body".
- **Don't use toxic positivity.** Avoid "just love yourself and it will work
  out", "summer body in 2 weeks".
- **Don't over-promise.** Avoid extreme claims like "drop 10kg in 10 days",
  "results for everyone, no effort".
- **Don't reduce them to a number** — always mention energy, confidence, health,
  not only kilos/sizes.
- **Don't use fear-mongering around health.** Avoid "you will get sick if you do
  not join now", "your body is a ticking time bomb".

## Emotional Progression

The progression a reader should feel as they move through our messaging:

1. **Validation** — the "exhale"
2. **Relief** — the "safety" (doctor / science involved)
3. **Permission** — the "yes" (okay to invest in myself)
4. **Excitement** — the "vision" (I can see myself in that dress / bikini)
